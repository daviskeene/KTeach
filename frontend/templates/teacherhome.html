About KTeach

KTeach is the Kotlin Classroom, dedicated to teaching others how to program with Kotlin. With KTeach, you can:

  • Create multiple classrooms
  • Create custom assignments
  • Make use of our custom testing framework to grade students
  • Make Kotlin a joyful learning experience
