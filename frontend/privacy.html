How KTeach keeps your private information safe.

How Your Data is Stored

The only information stored on our database is your Name, Email, Classroom Code, Complete Assignments and Unique ID. This ID is an MD5 hash of your e-mail address and password. We cannot see your password, but we have access to your email to contact you and to the hash for authentication. When you sign in, we verify that a user with a matching hash (ID) exists in the database. If they do, then we sign you in. If not, then we'll let you know that some information was entered incorrectly.

To view all data stored on our end, take a look at your browser's localStorage after a successful login attempt. This is how we load page content, by essentially storing a local copy of your information on the client's side and loading from local storage. Other dynamic content is loaded from calls to our API with your ID and classroom code.
